## Description (max 4000 characters)

```
Peguei

Never miss the moment that already happened.

Peguei keeps a rolling video buffer running continuously from the moment you open the app: while you're watching the line, waiting for a bite (or waiting for any fast outdoor moment to happen), the camera is already recording the last several seconds in the background — without filling up your storage, since only the most recent time window is kept.

When the moment arrives, just tap the capture button to start recording. Peguei saves a clip that includes the seconds that had ALREADY happened before you tapped, plus everything you record until you tap again to stop — because in the moment, nobody has time to hit "record" before the fish bites.

HOW IT WORKS
- Open the app and leave the camera running during your activity.
- The video buffer runs on its own, always, with nothing to turn on.
- When something worth keeping happens, tap the capture button to start recording.
- Tap again to stop — the saved clip already includes the "before" and "after" of your tap, with audio.

BUILT FOR FISHING — AND ANY OUTDOOR SPORT
Designed for anglers who want to capture the catch without missing the timing, but works just as well for hiking, surfing, skating, cycling, or any outdoor activity where the best moments happen too fast to start recording from scratch.

CONFIGURABLE YOUR WAY
- Adjustable video buffer window (15 to 60 seconds)
- Video quality in 720p, 1080p, or 4K
- Built-in gallery to review, share, or delete your saved clips

100% FREE
Peguei is free to use, with no subscription and no ads in this version.

PRIVACY
Peguei records locally on your device. Saved clips stay stored on your device — you decide when to share them.

Download Peguei and stop missing what already happened.
```
